KALDEWEI Load Carrier Traceability

Indicative offer structure for internal logistics based on two implementation options on the FORMATION platform.

Prepared for: KALDEWEI Date: 19.01.2026 Focus: Solution overview and indicative annual pricing

FORMATION proposes two implementation options to track approximately 3,000 load carriers across the defined storage and logistics areas at KALDEWEI. Both options share the same platform, asset model, and user workflow, while differing in positioning technology, accuracy, and integration depth.

1. Executive Summary

Commercial framing

KALDEWEI has a clear choice between a faster, lower-complexity QR-based solution and a more precise UWB-supported solution with mandatory SAP integration. Both options are built on the same FORMATION operating layer and remain within a comparable annual price range.

Shared platform basis

  • One FORMATION workspace and one asset model.
  • Identical check-in and check-out workflows for users.
  • Approximately 3,000 load carriers in scope.

Decision criteria

  • Required positioning precision.
  • Need for direct SAP process linkage.
  • Preferred balance between rollout speed and system depth.

StrengthsWorks indoors and outdoors, supports manual correction, and is quick to introduce.Best fit where approximate positioning is sufficient to reduce search time.

Indicative total for Option 1: EUR 36K per year.

Option 2 extends the same workflow with UWB-supported positioning and a mandatory SAP integration layer. In this setup, scan events and position data are transferred into SAP and linked to material and logistics processes there. Without SAP integration, this option is not operationally meaningful.
